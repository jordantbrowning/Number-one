North American River Otter

River otters love to play, whether in the water or on land!

  • Common Name: North American River Otter
  • Scientific Name: Lontra canadensis
  • Type: Mammal
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Average Life Span In The Wild: 8 to 9 years
  • Size: 33.5 to 52 in
  • Weight: 11 to 30 lbs

From National Geographic: The playful North American river otter is equally at home in the water and on land. It makes its home in a burrow near the water's edge, and can thrive in river, lake, swamp, or estuary ecosystems.
